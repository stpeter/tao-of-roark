The Tao of Roark

Suggestions for Further Exploration

If you enjoyed The Tao of Roark, here are some other works you might like:

  • The Fountainhead by Ayn Rand
  • Tao Te Ching by Lao Tzu (my preferred translation is Derek Lin's)
  • The Gnostic Gospels by Elaine Pagels
  • Old Nick's Guide to Happiness by Nicholas Dykes
  • The Gay Science by Friedrich Nietzsche
  • My book Letters on Happiness: An Epicurean Dialogue
  • Goldberg Variations by J.S. Bach (my favorite recordings are Glenn Gould's 1981 version and a transcription for harp by Catrin Finch)
